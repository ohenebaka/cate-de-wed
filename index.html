Company Profile

Cate de wed

Cate de wed is one of the catering and wedding services organization in Ghana. The organization which is a joint venture was established on 15th March, 2018 by Eric Odai Laryea

( Chief Executive Officer ), Bismark Anning ( Marketing Department ),

Aziz Abdul-Latif ( Fashion Department ), Vincent Gador (Decorations Manager), Victoria Konadu ( Food Manager ) and Ebenezer Tuffour ( Accounting and Planning Department ).

Our mission is to create lasting values for Ghanaians and meeting their expectations which is made possible by the expertise of our staff. We aim to promoting our culture as Ghanaians with what we do by providing you with simple but classic weddings embellished with our culture.

We serve the purpose of reducing the stress and the high cost incurred during the preparations for weddings in Ghana at a low cost. We provide you with catering services, decoration of receptions, gemming of brides and grooms in terms of their physical appearance for either your traditional or white weddings.
